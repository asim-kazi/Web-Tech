Our Story

Welcome to Hotel Adi – A Celebration of Pure Vegetarian Excellence

Since our inception on 28th December 2013, Hotel Adi has grown into a trusted name in pure vegetarian dining across Maharashtra. With roots in tradition and a vision for innovation, we offer a rich blend of culinary experiences, exceptional service, and tranquil ambiance.

A Destination for Memorable Gatherings

Our flagship location in Jaisingpur features a beautiful open garden restaurant, perfect for relaxed family meals or intimate evenings under the stars. With a seating capacity of 200 guests, and a spacious party hall that accommodates up to 300 people, Hotel Adi is the ideal venue for celebrations, events, and special gatherings.

Culinary Craftsmanship

We proudly serve a wide variety of vegetarian delights, including popular Italian and Punjabi cuisines, among many others—prepared with fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and a passion for flavor.

Expanding Across Maharashtra

In addition to our Jaisingpur establishment, Hotel Adi also has:

  • An open garden restaurant in Miraj, Maharashtra
  • Full-service restaurants in both Jaisingpur and Miraj
  • Chic, cozy cafés located in Jaisingpur, Ichalkaranji, and Kollapur

Our Core Promise

Every Hotel Adi location is built around our core promise: delivering pure vegetarian food in warm, welcoming spaces designed for memorable experiences.

Join Us

Come dine with us at our Jaisingpur location and experience hospitality rooted in tradition, served with a modern touch.
